About Psyche AI LLC

Psyche AI LLC is a technology company focused on building software applications that support mental clarity, emotional wellness, and intentional living.

Our mission is to help people reconnect with their authentic selves through thoughtfully designed digital tools. We believe technology should support human flourishing—helping users find clarity, reduce mental noise, and make decisions aligned with their values.

Founded with a commitment to ethical design, we prioritize user wellbeing over engagement metrics. Our products are designed to be calming, non-addictive, and genuinely helpful.
