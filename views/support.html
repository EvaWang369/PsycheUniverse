Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?
To restore your purchase, open the SubliminalGen app and go to Settings. Tap "Restore Purchases" and sign in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase. Your subscription and any previous purchases will be restored automatically.
How do subscriptions work?
SubliminalGen offers auto-renewable subscription plans including VIP Gold (1,200 seconds per billing cycle) and VIP Platinum (18,000 seconds per billing cycle). Your generation seconds reset at the beginning of each billing period. Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
How do I cancel my subscription?
To cancel your subscription, open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad, tap your name at the top, then tap "Subscriptions." Find SubliminalGen in the list and tap "Cancel Subscription." Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.
Why is my AI music generation not working?
If AI music generation isn't working, please check the following: ensure you have an active internet connection, verify that you have remaining generation seconds in your plan, and try restarting the app. If the issue persists, please contact our support team at cs@psyche-ai.xyz with details about the error you're experiencing.
How do I contact support?
You can reach our support team by emailing cs@psyche-ai.xyz. Please include your account email and a detailed description of your issue. We typically respond within 24–48 hours.
